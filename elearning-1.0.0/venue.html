The Venue

Kaohsiung Marriott Hotel

PACIS 2028 will be hosted at the Kaohsiung Marriott Hotel, a state-of-the-art venue opened in 2020 that combines world-class conference facilities with luxury accommodations. Conveniently integrated with shopping malls, restaurants, and medical facilities, the hotel offers everything delegates need within easy reach.

11 meeting rooms

Main hall for 1000 attendees

Opened in 2020

Luxury accommodations on-site
