The Host City

Discover Kaohsiung

Kaohsiung is Taiwan's vibrant harbour city — a key economic and industrial hub blending deep maritime heritage with rapid modernization. With stunning harbour views, a diverse urban landscape, and a strong commitment to sustainability, the city offers an inspiring backdrop for PACIS 2028.

From world-class art districts and waterfront landmarks to mountain skywalks and island beaches, Kaohsiung invites delegates to explore its food, culture, and leisure between conference sessions.

Food, Culture & Leisure

Kaohsiung Attractions

Harbour & Yancheng Area
The Pier-2 Art Center

A vibrant waterfront arts district set in repurposed harbour warehouses.

Kaohsiung Music Center

Striking waterfront concert venue on Love River Bay — steps from Pier-2.

Love River

The romantic riverside heart of the city, beautiful by day and night.

Cijin Beach

An island beach reached by a short ferry ride from the harbour, known for seafood and sunsets.

City Centre
Sunfong Temple

A historic temple rich in local folk culture, in the city centre.

Dream Mall

One of Taiwan's largest shopping malls, crowned by a Ferris wheel.

Northern Kaohsiung — Near HSR Zuoying
Lotus Pond (Lianchihtan)

Scenic lake area famed for the Dragon and Tiger Pagodas, near Zuoying HSR station.

Day Trips & Outskirts
Siaogangshan Skywalk Park

A skywalk offering sweeping views over the surrounding landscape.

Fo Guang Shan

A magnificent Buddhist monastery and the Buddha Museum, about 40 min from the city.

Tianliao Moon World

Otherworldly badlands terrain shaped by erosion, about 50 min from the city.

After Dark

Night Markets & Waterfront

Explore Kaohsiung's famous night markets and waterfront attractions — a must-do for any visitor.

六合夜市 (Liuhe Night Market)

Kaohsiung's most iconic night market, located in the city centre near the main train station. Famous for papaya milk, grilled seafood, and traditional Taiwanese snacks. Open every evening.

瑞豐夜市 (Ruifeng Night Market)

One of the largest night markets in southern Taiwan, packed with local street food, games, and stalls. Particularly popular with students and families. Located near Zuoying MRT station.

光華夜市 (Guanghua Night Market)

A lively neighbourhood night market with authentic local flavours, less touristy than Liuhe and beloved by residents for its variety of hot pot, barbecue, and traditional desserts.

興中夜市 (Xingzhong Night Market)

A neighbourhood market well-known among locals for affordable eats and a relaxed atmosphere, located in the Sanmin District near the city centre.
