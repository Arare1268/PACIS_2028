About

What is PACIS?

The annual Pacific Asia Conference on Information Systems (PACIS) is one of the leading conferences on information systems and the only Association for Information Systems (AIS) conference dedicated to the Pacific Asia Region. PACIS is endorsed by the AIS Council and governed by the AIS Region 3 Board. Full proceedings of all past PACIS can be downloaded from the AIS eLibrary.

Kaohsiung Marriott Hotel, Kaohsiung, Taiwan

Hosted by the Department of Information Management, NSYSU

July 4–8, 2028

Conference Theme

AI — Its Risks and Opportunities

PACIS 2028 spotlights the transformative role of Artificial Intelligence in information systems and society, exploring both its opportunities and its risks in building a more sustainable society. Conference tracks will be confirmed as the program develops.
