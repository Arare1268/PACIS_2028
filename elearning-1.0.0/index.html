PACIS 2028

Welcome to PACIS 2028

PACIS 2028 will be held in the vibrant and inclusive harbor city of Kaohsiung, Taiwan. Spotlighting the critical role of technology in societal transformation under the theme, “Preparing The Next Generation For The IT-Driven Future”.

As the world enters a new era of digital transformation, the event will discuss how educational innovation and technological integration can empower the youth to lead in an ever-evolving tech landscape. PACIS 2028 seeks to foster dynamic international discussions and collaborations that harness technological potential to overcome global challenges and push forward our societies toward a smarter, more sustainable, and inclusive future. Kaohsiung, Taiwan’s key economic and industrial hub, sets an energetic stage for this year’s conference. Known for its deep maritime heritage and rapid modernization, Kaohsiung is a gateway to Asia’s thriving digital markets. The city’s recent focus on 5G AIoT innovation and its burgeoning semiconductor ecosystem underscore its importance as a regional hub for growth. With its stunning harbor views, diverse urban landscape, and commitment to sustainability, Kaohsiung offers an inspiring environment for participants to explore transformative digital paths that drive progress.

Top researchers, practitioners, and thought leaders will gather in Kaohsiung from July 4th to 8th, 2028, to present the latest research, share insights, and engage in forward-thinking dialogue. With its vibrant harbor energy and dedication to progress, Kaohsiung provides an ideal environment to collaborate on shaping IT-driven solutions that empower communities and create a lasting impact in the digital age.
