Getting Around

Reaching the Conference Venue

Kaohsiung Int'l Airport (KHH)

Station R4 on the KMRT Red Line — a direct metro ride to the city centre and the venue.

HSR Zuoying Station

Station R16. Taipei → Zuoying takes about 2.5 hours by High-Speed Rail.

Kaohsiung Marriott Hotel

KMRT/Light Rail stations R13 / C24, with the venue connected to metro, rail, and highways.

From Abroad

International Visitor Routes

Via Kaohsiung Airport (KHH)

Take the KMRT Red Line from R4 to R10 for the city centre — the fastest option for delegates flying directly into Kaohsiung.

Via Taipei Airport (TPE)

1. Metro from TPE to HSR Taoyuan Station.

2. High-Speed Rail from Taoyuan to Zuoying.

3. KMRT Red Line from R16 to R10.

Participants from Oceania can transfer through Hong Kong or other regional hubs.
